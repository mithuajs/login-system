About Institute

Sylhet Polytechnic Institute (SPI) is one of the oldest and most prestigious government technical educational institutions in Bangladesh, renowned for delivering quality mid-level engineering and technical education. Established in 1955, the institute operates under the executive control of the Directorate of Technical Education (DTE) and is academically affiliated with the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB). Located at Technical Road, Boroikandi, South Surma, Sylhet—just about one kilometer from the main Sylhet Railway Station and close to the Kadamtoli Bus Terminal—the institute sits at a highly accessible and central hub of the region. Spanning a lush, serene 17.83-acre campus, SPI provides a well-rounded academic atmosphere featuring a three-story administrative and academic building, a dedicated computer building, 16 specialized workshops, 12 modern laboratories, a central library, a spacious playground, a central mosque, student hostels, and a scenic campus pond surrounded by greenery.

The institute primarily offers four-year Diploma-in-Engineering programs across two academic shifts (First Shift and Second Shift) to accommodate a large number of aspiring technicians and engineers. Students can pursue specialized studies in seven core technology departments: Civil Technology, Electrical Technology, Mechanical Technology, Computer Science & Technology (CST), Electronics Technology, Power Technology, and Electromedical Technology. In addition to technical courses, a dedicated Non-Tech Department imparts essential training in basic sciences, mathematics, humanities, and English language skills to ensure holistic development. Admission to these programs is strictly merit-based, handled through BTEB's centralized online admission process following the completion of Secondary School Certificate (SSC) or equivalent examinations. Over the decades, Sylhet Polytechnic Institute has played a crucial role in empowering youth with practical skills, producing skilled engineering technicians who contribute significantly to industrial development both in Bangladesh and abroad.
